## Cultural Sensitivity

### Pronunciation
**Rowshni:** ROHSH-nee

Always include pronunciation guide when:
- Introducing the brand for first time
- In About/Brand pages
- In presentations
- In user guides

### Name Origin

**Celebrate the meaning:**
- The name "Rowshni" means "light" (used across multiple languages including Urdu, Hindi, and Persian)
- Focus on the universal symbol of "light" representing clarity and illumination
- Use lantern/lamp imagery as a symbolic connection
- Emphasize the meaning, not the specific language origin

**Be respectful:**
- Pronounce correctly in videos/demos (ROHSH-nee)
- Include diverse representation in marketing
- Celebrate the universal appeal of "light" across cultures
- Avoid claiming exclusive ownership of the word to any single language

## Don'ts

**âŒ Don't:**
- Use generic light bulb emoji without context
- Overuse light metaphors to point of clichÃ©

- Use aggressive sales language
- Make fun of accountants or the profession
- Use dated accounting stereotypes
- Forget to explain the name meaning
- Skip pronunciation guides
